Ziheng Wu

Welcome! I'm Ziheng Wu, currently a research assistant at AI for Thermofluid Physics Lab, Peking University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhi Chen.

I obtained my B.Eng. in Energy and Power Engineering at Harbin Engineering University. After graduation, I completed my M.Sc. in Power Engineering and Engineering Thermophysics at Beihang University, where I was co-supervised by Prof. Chi Zhang and Prof. Bosen Wang. Currently, my research interests primarily lie in AI, HPC(High Performance Computing) and combustion.
